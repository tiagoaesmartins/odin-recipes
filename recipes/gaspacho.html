Gaspacho "Alentejana"

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 100g crushed fresh tomatoes
  • 300g minced fresh tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 150g minced green bell pepper
  • 150g minced cucumber
  • 1,5l fresh water OR 1l fresh water and 5dl ice
  • coriander and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix the garlic cloves with the salt and the crushed tomatoes until you get a liquid paste.
  2. Pour that paste in the bowl where the dish is to be served, and add the olive oil, the vinegar, and the oregano.
  3. Add the minced tomatoes, green bell pepper, and cucumber.
  4. Mix all and add the water.
  5. Add the coriander and black pepper, and adjust the vinegar.

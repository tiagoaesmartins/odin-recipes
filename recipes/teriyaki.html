Teriyaki Tofu Marinade

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c cold water
  • 1/3 c brown sugar
  • 1/4 c soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • minced garlic, ginger, chili to taste

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a pan.
  2. Simmer over medium heat until all of the sugar dissolves.
  3. Pour over your favorite protein (we highly recommend high protein tofu cut into 2cm cubes) and let it marinate for, at least, 15 minutes.
  4. Pour everything back into the pan, and let it cook until the sauce is thick and gooey.
